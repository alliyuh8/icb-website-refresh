We Are Invention Corps
An interdisciplinary group of students driven to create meaningful global impact through human-centered design.
We Do Many Types of Design

From redesigning web interfaces to prototyping physical products, our members are passionate in all sorts of different design projects. We dive headfirst into the design process with clients from a diverse array of industries.

We Have Four Impact Pillars

We combine our passion for design with our tenacity for social good in the realms of society, environment, health, and poverty. These four pillars serve as the crucial framework for the designs we create.

We Are Made Up Of Thinkers, Tinkerers, Creators, and Problem-Solvers

With members majoring in architecture, engineering, environmental studies, political science, CS, and more, we believe the best work and the best teams are multidisciplinary. Our recruitment process is focused not on your major or experience level, but how you creatively think about and approach problems.

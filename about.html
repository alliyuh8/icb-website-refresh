Core Values

We are a diverse organization united by a shared passion for positive impact. By fostering collaboration, open-mindedness, and empathy, we empower individuals to grow and contribute to a community rooted in a commitment to make a difference.

Founding Story
Invention Corps was founded in 2017 by the University Innovation Fellows, as part of an effort to increase undergraduate innovation and entrepreneurship within UC Berkeley.
Since our beginnings, we have always championed multidisciplinary designers, thinkers, and leaders who seek to tackle the “wicked problems” of society through human-centered design. Unlike other design consulting organizations, we offer pro-bono partnerships and high quality deliverables as part of our dedication toward positive social impact.
Because of this, we have no formal recruitment process (resumes, portfolios, etc.) and instead gauge creative and collaborative skills through design challenges. Likewise, our club is completely operated by students, and each member has equal responsibility and agency in sustaining and improving our community.
